Tri-Valley Readers

Where stories bring us together.

Welcome to Tri-Valley Readers! We are a women-only book club based in the Tri-Valley Region of the East Bay, but ladies from all corners of the Bay Area are welcome to join.

We meet on a monthly basis and read everything including fantasy, feminist literature, gothic horror, historical romance, and beyond! We draw upon recommendations from our members and are always open to new suggestions.

What We Do

Monthly meetings

We gather once a month to discuss that month's book. Meetings are relaxed and inclusive.

Diverse picks

Our reading list rotates between literary fiction, nonfiction, and occasional classics or genre favorites. Members suggest and vote on upcoming titles.

Community

Beyond the books, we're looking to build a real community. Our goal is to foster an environment for women who love reading and want to bond with others who share their passion.

Head over to our Reading List to see what we're reading now and what we've read in the past, or check out Membership for how to join (it's free!).
